Buc-ee’s: The Largest Gas Station on planet Earth (in New Braunfels, Texas)

Buc-ee’s convenience store and gas station chain has 49 locations in Alabama, Colorado, Florida, Texas, Georgia, Kentucky, Missouri, South Carolina and Tennessee, USA. A Buc-ee’s gas station in Texas holds three world records: the station with the most pumps, the world’s longest car wash and the largest gas station store on the planet.
